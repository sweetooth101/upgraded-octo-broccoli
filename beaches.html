Hermosa Beach

Hermosa Beach is the least formal of the bunch of the southbay, a place where you're more likely to see a skateboarder than a spa. Of the South Bay towns, it has the smallest population and is perfect for unwinding in typical SoCal style: casual wear, lounging at the beach, and that perfect California weather.

Sunset Beach

On breezy days, expert kiteboarders will be zigzagging around out in the surf. On sunny days, sunbathers enjoy this beach which is a bit quieter than the other HB beaches. Your views will be big ship containers, they are far but great to see.
